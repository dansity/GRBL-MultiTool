CNC Drilling Path G-Code Generator

Generates G-Code (G81/G73 emulation) for drilling multiple holes with optional chip-clearing pecker cycle.

Drilling Parameters

Total depth to drill.

Plunge Depth (Z) is the total desired depth of the drilled hole, specified as a positive value. It translates to a negative Z coordinate.

Feed rate for G1 moves.

Plunge Speed (F) is the speed (feed rate) at which the drill moves vertically (down) when cutting into the material.

Time in milliseconds (0 for none).

Dwell Time (G4) is a pause commanded at the bottom of the hole, to ensure the hole bottom is clear. Specified in milliseconds.

Z height for rapid moves (G0).

Safe Z Clearance is the Z-height above the material used for rapid horizontal travel (G0) between holes or during chip clearing.

Minimum 2 pecks.

Number of Pecks divides the total plunge depth into equal segments. After each peck, the tool retracts to the top to clear chips.

Enter X and Y coordinates separated by a comma (or space/tab).

Drill Hole Locations are the X and Y coordinates of the center of each hole, relative to the work zero (G90). Enter one coordinate pair per line.
